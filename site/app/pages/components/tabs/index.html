Tabs make it easy to explore, switch between different views and enable content organization at a high level, such as different data sets or functional aspects of an app.

Navigational tabs

Sometimes you may need a tab to be the main navigational element of your application and you can do this. Tabs integrate with UI Router by default and will be able to use single tab just like a regular button or link, by using the to attribute.
